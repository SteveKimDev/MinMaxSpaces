About Us

It's Been Our Driving Force

MinMaxSpaces™ is a turn-key provider of steel-framed modular and container relocatable buildings and storage solutions that are designed to deliver a renewable living environment. MinMaxSpaces™ serves as agent and exclusive distributor for one of the world's leading modular manufacturers. Designs Building Units

  • Fast & Furious

    A fabricated building systems that interconnects and can be erected and operational in just hours not days or months and without any welding or the need for any tools.

  • Flexible

    FOBS are a modular building system with a series of fourteen (14) prefabricated, interchangeable, interlocking structurally-insulated wall panels creating unlimited building possibilities. MinMaxSpaces™ can be adjoined to create an even larger footprint and are even strong enough to be stacked upon themselves.

  • Custom Designs

    You can literally move windows and door locations around as desired when assembling. Wall panels are spray-foam insulated with different face material available from wood to metal creating unlimited design possibilities.

  • Logistics

    These units are broken down and shipped in a knockdown state, allowing for 4-8 units to fit in a single sea freight container, resulting in an unparalleled logistical and freight advantage.

  • Portability

    Structurally equivalent to an ISO shipping container MinMaxSpaces™ can be lifted by crane, or offloaded by zoom boom or forklift with a dry weight of approximately 5,000 pounds.

  • Standardized

    MinMaxSpaces™ share the same dimensions as standard 20ft ISO Shipping Containers. This standardization creates tremendous building opportunities from stack building units to a true vertical solution for your temporary apartment complex. These marvelous units put the MOD in modern & modular building solutions!!!

  • Built To USA Standards

    Floor and roof structures are insulated to an R25 value. The wall panels are all insulated to a minimum of R20 value and a re designed to keep you warm in cold weather and cool on warm days.

  • Certified

    Our FOBS are CSA, Intertek and UL certified. All welding is AWS certified. Units come equipped with CSA/UL electrical components and EnergyStar appliances.

  • Unlimited Applications

    Everything from Modular classrooms, site office complexes, security entrance gates, mobile accommodation solutions, disaster and refugee housing solutions to permanent commercial and residential applications.
